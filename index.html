About BeMyGuest

Welcome to BeMyGuest (BMG), your one-stop multiservice solution for connecting with friends and family, and accessing helpful services – all in one user-friendly app.

Hostings

Share Your Space Globally

Share your living space with trusted connections in your extended network. Enjoy the comfort and security of staying with people you know, creating a home-away-from-home experience.

Offerings

Buy, Sell, or Rent Anything

BMG is your virtual marketplace for exchanging items without any monetary transactions. Use BMG points to rent, buy, or sell items such as toys, cameras, TV sets, and more. It’s like a neighborhood garage sale, minus the hassle of negotiating with strangers.

Services

Lend or Get a Helping Hand

Whether you’re seeking assistance or have skills to share, BMG Services connects you with like-minded neighbors eager to contribute to the community. From pet sitting and personal training to carpooling and more, it's like having a helping hand right in your neighborhood whenever you need it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a community run by Karma. The more you give, the more you get. Embrace the joy of meaningful connections, live a life with memorable experiences and make the world accessible through a global BMG family.

And remember, our app operates on BMG points, not money. Host guests, refer friends, offer services, and post listings—all while making a positive impact on the community.
